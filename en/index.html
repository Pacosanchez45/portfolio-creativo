About

I’m Francisco Sánchez, a designer specialized in user interfaces (UI) and modern web layout. I’m passionate about transforming ideas into visual experiences that are clear, coherent, and functional. I work with tools like Figma for design and prototyping, and I develop with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, taking care of every detail from structure to interaction.

My approach combines aesthetics, usability, and purpose: I believe good design should not only look good but also communicate intuitively and inspire confidence in the person using it. I’m particularly interested in building consistent design systems, web typography, and subtle transitions that bring smoothness to each interface.

I’m always looking to improve, learn new trends, and maintain a clean, accessible, user-centered visual style. Each project is an opportunity to tell a story through design, balancing creativity with technical precision.
