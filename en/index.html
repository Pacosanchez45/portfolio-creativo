How I work

A simple, repeatable, product-oriented process.

1) Understand the problem

Goals, users, constraints, and what “success” means for the product.

2) Design the flow + UI

Wireframes, hierarchy, states, components, and a Figma prototype.

3) Validate and deliver

Responsive design + basic accessibility + handoff (or partial implementation).

About

I’m Francisco Sánchez. I focus on product UI/UX and turning designs into production-ready interfaces. I work with Figma (Auto Layout, variants) and build with HTML/CSS and JavaScript, taking care of structure, hierarchy, interaction, and basic accessibility.

I’m especially interested in building consistent systems (components/states), improving flow clarity, and reducing friction on key screens.
