How I work

A simple process to move from “this is unclear” to “now it works”.

I adapt to where your company is: a website that needs redesign, a landing page that needs to sell better, an internal tool that should be easier to use, or an idea that still needs shape.

01

Diagnosis

I understand goals, users, content, competitors and friction points in the current experience.

02

Visual direction

I define structure, hierarchy, visual tone, components, responsive behavior and key states.

03

Useful delivery

I prepare Figma design, clear handoff and, if needed, clean Front-End implementation.

About

I design with an eye on how a screen looks, how it reads and how it gets built.

I like combining visual judgment with Front-End execution. That helps me design realistic, consistent interfaces that are ready to work in a product, not just look good in a screenshot.

I use Figma to structure flows, components and prototypes, and HTML, CSS and JavaScript to bring those decisions into a responsive, clear and polished experience.
